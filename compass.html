Compass App

Compass is an app concept for cyclists to find and share alternative ways of navigating an environment.

Cyclists create their own routes that they navigate daily. These routes are often quicker than documented bike paths.

The app gives riders the ability to share their routes and use routes uploaded by users. The goal is to help cyclists more efficiently ride to their destination and connect the cyclingÂ community.
