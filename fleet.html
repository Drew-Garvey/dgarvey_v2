Fleet App

Workplaces are often plagued with issues surrounding task management, scheduling, and reporting. Fleet is an app concept that solves these issues and improve workflow.

With Fleet, an employer can assign their employees tasks to complete for the day or week. As an employee completes tasks, the app records this and delivers accurate reporting. The reports are visible to both the employer and employee in order to alleviate conflicts and promote transparency.

The app also streamlines communication through a chat function that links into a calendar. Employees are able to see other employees’ calendars in order to make scheduling more clear. This minimizes work lost for a company and promotes workplace cohesion.
