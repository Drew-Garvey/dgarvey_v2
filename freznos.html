Freznos Tea

Freznos a self iterated brand identity project, with the goal to explore odd combinations of tea.

I started with the creation of the mark. I made a logotype by combining 2 typefaces; Futura and ITC Avant Garde. The Futura represents the more serious and structured part of the brand culture. The ITC Avant Garde is more humanistic and playful which exemplifies the fun and experimental side of the brand.

The style guide gives strict instructions about the use of the brand on the stationary system, packaging and vehicles. The book is printed on a thick, matte stock and is hand stitched.
