Ocean Art Website

Ocean Art is a small frame shop in Virginia Beach. Together Aaron Wentt and I partnered to redesign the website. We both split the tasks of design and development to accomplish this.

Our goals were to simplify the look of the website and make it responsive for users on the go. We also included features such as the Google Maps API to help customers get directions to the store's location.
