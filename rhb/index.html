ABOUT

Raymond P. Sarsaparilla is a world renouned cook who specializes in all things meat. He spent the majority of his life competing, and winning countless pit barbecue competitions.

In 1966, Ray decided to slow down and open Ray's Hell Burger, with a goal of simple American food served hot.

Today his two sons, Ray Jr. and Robert, run the shop with the same menu and recipes that Ray started out with.
