GMU Senior Show Site

At the end of every semester, the senior art students George Mason University put on a senior show. Chris Sullivan and I were tasked with creating a website for the show.

The header of the site is an illustration that Crystal Au made for the corresponding poster for the show. It contains the date, time and location of the show.

The section below the header

The style guide gives strict instructions about the use of the brand on the stationary system, packaging and vehicles. The book is printed on a thick, matte stock and is hand stitched.
